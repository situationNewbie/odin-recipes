The Cannolo

Example of presentation for cannolo

The cannolo is fried pastry of sicilian culture.
It's a conical sweet fried pastry filled with ricotta cream and decorated with sweet fruits zest or pistachio or chocolate

Ingretients

  • cannolo zest
  • ricotta cream
  • Decoration elements

Steps:

  1. Fill the cannolo zest with the ricotta cream
  2. decorate the cannolo with your choosed ingridient
