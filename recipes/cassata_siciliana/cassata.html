The Sicilian Cassata

Example of presentation for sicilian cassata

The cassata is a pastry of sicilian culture.
It's a cake filled with ricotta cream and sponge cake covered with pistachio flavored almond paste decorated with sweet fruits zest.

Ingretients

  • almond paste
  • ricotta cream
  • sponge cake
  • Decoration elements

Steps:

  1. cover the bottom of a pan with a layer of stretched almond paste
  2. alternate ricotta cream with sponge cake
  3. when you will reach the top line put a strerched layr of almonde paste
  4. flip out the cake
  5. decorate the top of the cake with sweets fruits zest and serve it.
