The Lasagna

Example of presentation for lasagna

The lasagna is a kind of pasta./n The most know recipe is Lasagne alla bolognese one of the best dish of italian culture. /n It's cooked and oven's baked dish.

Ingretients

  • pasta lasagna
  • bolognese souce
  • besciamella

Steps:

  1. in a pan add bolognese sauce on the bottom
  2. cover the souse with a layer of pasta lasagna already cooked
  3. spread with besciamela on top
  4. repeat the first 3 steps till the top of the pan
  5. bake in oven for 30 min. at 220 grade C
